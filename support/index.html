TableTurnr Support Center

Help for setup, integrations, billing, and technical issues.

Contact Support

Need direct assistance? Email us at support@tableturnr.com.

Typical response time: within 48 hours on business days.

Quick Help Topics

POS Integration Help

For Square, Toast, Clover, and Lightspeed setup support, include your restaurant name and connected POS provider in your support email.

Account Setup and Access

If you are unable to sign in, include your account email and any error message. We can assist with onboarding, roles, and location-level access.

Billing and Subscription Questions

For plan, invoice, or renewal questions, email support with your account email and organization name so we can locate your subscription quickly.

Report a Bug

When reporting an issue, please include:

  • Device type and OS version
  • App version (if available)
  • A short description of what happened
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots if possible
