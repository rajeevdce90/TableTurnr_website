Case Study

IHOP Franchise Network

How a multi-location IHOP franchise cut daily reporting time by 80% and gained real-time visibility with TableTurnr.

The Challenge

A multi-location IHOP franchise was managing sales reporting, labor scheduling, and inventory tracking across disconnected systems - spreadsheets, multiple POS logins, and manual supplier calls. Managers spent 3+ hours daily reconciling data, and ownership had limited visibility into real-time performance across locations.

The Solution

TableTurnr was deployed across the franchise locations, connecting directly to their existing POS and supplier systems. Key modules activated:

  • Real-time sales dashboards replacing manual Excel tracking
  • Staff utilization and coverage analytics replacing guesswork scheduling
  • Multi-location comparison giving ownership a single pane of glass across all stores
  • RevPath heatmaps identifying peak revenue hours and underperforming dayparts

The Results

80% Reduction in daily reporting time
Real-time Visibility across all franchise locations
AI-optimized Staffing aligned to actual demand
Faster Decision-making in real-time
