Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

TableTurnr uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the platform to function. These handle authentication, session management, and security. You cannot opt out of these.
  • Analytics cookies: We use Google Analytics (property ID: G-3MS0DYYYXY) to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve page performance and content.
  • Functional cookies: These remember your preferences such as language settings or display options to provide a more personalized experience.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services we use:

  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymized usage data including pages visited, session duration, and device type. Google's privacy policy applies to their processing of this data.
  • Calendly: Used on our demo booking page. Calendly may set cookies when you interact with the scheduling widget.
  • Font Awesome CDN: May set cookies when loading icon fonts. These are limited to content delivery purposes.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete existing cookies
  • Block all or specific cookies
  • Set preferences for certain websites

Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of the TableTurnr platform from functioning correctly.

5. Cookie Duration

Session cookies are temporary and deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. Analytics cookies typically expire after 6 to 24 months depending on the provider.

6. Your Consent

By continuing to use the TableTurnr website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or contacting us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy when we add new services or change how we use tracking technologies. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies, contact us at support@tableturnr.com.
